Exercise 11.1 – Division of a Line Segment

Q1. Divide a line segment AB in a given ratio m:n

Steps:
1. Draw AB
2. From A, draw ray at an acute angle
3. Mark m+n equal divisions on ray
4. Connect last division point to B
5. Draw parallel lines from mth point to intersect AB → division point P
Then AP/PB = m/n

Exercise 11.2 – Construction of Tangents

Q1. Draw tangent to a circle from external point P

Steps:
1. Draw circle with center O
2. Join OP
3. Find midpoint M of OP
4. Draw circle with center M and radius MP
5. Intersect original circle at points A and B
6. Draw PA and PB → tangents

Exercise 11.3 – Triangle Constructions

Q1. Construct ΔABC given base BC, sum of other two sides, and altitude from vertex

Steps:
1. Draw base BC
2. Draw perpendicular from midpoint of base with given altitude
3. Draw arcs from B and C with radii equal to given sides sum
4. Intersection gives vertex A
5. Join AB and AC → ΔABC

Q2. Construct ΔABC given base BC, difference of sides, and median from vertex

Steps:
1. Draw base BC
2. Draw median from midpoint of base
3. Draw arcs from B and C using side lengths with difference condition
4. Intersection gives vertex A
5. Join AB and AC → ΔABC

Exercise 11.4 – Miscellaneous Constructions

Q1. Draw a triangle similar to a given triangle with given scale factor

Steps:
1. Draw given triangle ABC
2. Draw a ray from vertex A
3. Mark divisions on ray proportional to scale factor
4. Join last division to opposite vertex
5. Draw parallel lines from other divisions → vertices of similar triangle

Q2. Construct triangle when perimeter and two angles are given

Steps:
1. Draw base using given perimeter info
2. Construct given angles at base vertices
3. Extend angle bisectors to meet → third vertex
4. Join vertices to complete triangle

Summary – Constructions

  • Divide line segment in given ratio
  • Draw tangents from external point
  • Construct triangles using sides, angles, medians, altitudes
  • Construct similar triangles using scale factor
  • Use arcs, perpendiculars, and parallel lines for accuracy
