Surface Areas and Volumes

Exercise 13.1 • 13.2 • 13.3 • 13.4 • 13.5

Exercise 12.1 – Surface Area of Solids

Q1. Surface area of a cube of side 5 cm

Surface area of cube = 6 × side² = 6 × 5² = 6 × 25 = 150 cm²

Q2. Surface area of cuboid 10×5×4 cm

Surface area = 2(lb + bh + hl)
= 2(10×5 + 5×4 + 4×10) = 2(50 + 20 + 40) = 2×110 = 220 cm²

Exercise 12.2 – Lateral Surface Area of Cylinder, Cone, Sphere

Q1. LSA of cylinder radius 7 cm, height 10 cm

LSA = 2πrh = 2×π×7×10 = 140π ≈ 439.82 cm²

Q2. LSA of cone radius 3 cm, slant height 5 cm

LSA = πrl = π×3×5 = 15π ≈ 47.12 cm²

Exercise 12.3 – Total Surface Area

Q1. TSA of sphere radius 7 cm

TSA = 4πr² = 4×π×7² = 196π ≈ 615.75 cm²

Q2. TSA of cylinder radius 3 cm, height 10 cm

TSA = 2πr(h+r) = 2×π×3(10+3) = 6×13π = 78π ≈ 245.04 cm²

Exercise 12.4 – Volume of Solids

Q1. Volume of cube side 5 cm

V = side³ = 5³ = 125 cm³

Q2. Volume of cuboid 10×5×4 cm

V = l × b × h = 10 × 5 × 4 = 200 cm³

Q3. Volume of cylinder radius 7 cm, height 10 cm

V = πr²h = π×7²×10 = 490π ≈ 1539.38 cm³

Exercise 12.5 – Miscellaneous Problems

Q1. Water in a cylinder 14 cm diameter, 24 cm high

Radius r = 7 cm, h = 24 cm
Volume = πr²h = π×7²×24 = 1176π ≈ 3692.16 cm³

Q2. Volume of cone radius 3 cm, height 4 cm

V = 1/3 π r² h = 1/3 × π × 3² × 4 = 12π ≈ 37.70 cm³

Q3. Sphere radius 7 cm

V = 4/3 π r³ = 4/3 × π × 7³ = 1372/3 π ≈ 1436.76 cm³

Summary – Surface Areas and Volumes

  • Cube: LSA = 4a², TSA = 6a², V = a³
  • Cuboid: LSA = 2h(l+b), TSA = 2(lb+bh+hl), V = l×b×h
  • Cylinder: LSA = 2πrh, TSA = 2πr(h+r), V = πr²h
  • Cone: LSA = πrl, TSA = πr(l+r), V = 1/3 πr²h
  • Sphere: TSA = 4πr², V = 4/3 πr³
