Section A: Multiple Choice Questions (1 × 20 = 20)

Q1.

Which of the following is not a greenhouse gas? (a) CO₂ (b) Methane (c) Oxygen (d) Nitrous oxide

Q2.

Which part of the plant cell contains chlorophyll? (a) Mitochondria (b) Chloroplast (c) Nucleus (d) Ribosome

Q3.

The SI unit of force is: (a) Newton (b) Joule (c) Watt (d) Pascal

Q4.

Which vitamin is required for blood clotting? (a) Vitamin A (b) Vitamin K (c) Vitamin D (d) Vitamin C

Q5.

The lens of the human eye helps to: (a) Detect color (b) Adjust focus (c) Transmit signal (d) Produce tears

Q6.

Q7.

Which process in plants produces oxygen? (a) Respiration (b) Photosynthesis (c) Transpiration (d) Fermentation

Q8.

Which acid is present in lemon? (a) Citric acid (b) Acetic acid (c) Sulfuric acid (d) Hydrochloric acid

Q9.

Which organ filters blood in humans? (a) Heart (b) Kidney (c) Liver (d) Spleen

Q10.

The speed of light in vacuum is approximately: (a) 3 × 10⁸ m/s (b) 3 × 10⁶ m/s (c) 3 × 10⁵ m/s (d) 3 × 10⁷ m/s

Q11.

Which process involves division of nucleus into two identical nuclei? (a) Mitosis (b) Meiosis (c) Fertilization (d) Pollination

Q12.

Which gas is released during aerobic respiration? (a) Oxygen (b) Carbon dioxide (c) Nitrogen (d) Hydrogen

Q13.

Which device converts electrical energy into mechanical energy? (a) Electric motor (b) Cell (c) Transformer (d) Generator

Q14.

The phenomenon of splitting white light into colors is called: (a) Reflection (b) Dispersion (c) Refraction (d) Diffraction

Q15.

Which of the following is a non-metal? (a) Copper (b) Sulfur (c) Iron (d) Aluminium

Q16.

Which part of the brain controls voluntary actions? (a) Cerebellum (b) Medulla (c) Cerebrum (d) Spinal cord

Q17.

The process by which plants lose water is called: (a) Transpiration (b) Respiration (c) Photosynthesis (d) Evaporation

Q18.

Which component of blood transports oxygen? (a) RBC (b) WBC (c) Platelets (d) Plasma

Q19.

Which law relates voltage, current, and resistance? (a) Faraday's law (b) Ohm's law (c) Newton’s law (d) Hooke’s law

Q20.

Which gas causes acid rain? (a) CO₂ (b) SO₂ (c) O₂ (d) N₂
